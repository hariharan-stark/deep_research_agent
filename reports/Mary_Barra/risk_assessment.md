A significant portion of her net worth is attributed to her GM stock holdings and strategic investments [Mirror Review](https://blog.mirrorreview.com/mary-barra-net-worth/), [BusinessWomen](https://businesswomen.com/profiles/mary-barra/). Beyond GM, she was elected to The Walt Disney Company Board of Directors on August 24, 2017, a role for which she earned $361,657 in cash [Disney Newsroom](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/mary-t-barra-general-motors-co-chairman-ceo-elected-walt-disney-company-board-directors-2/), [Phoebe Wall Howard](https://phoebewallhoward.substack.com/p/can-mary-barra-helm-gm-while-juggling).
