### 1. Deep Fact Extraction

*   **Biographical details (verified)**:
    Satya Narayana Nadella was born on August 19, 1967 [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella). He is an Indian-American business executive [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella). Nadella earned a master's degree in computer science from the University of Wisconsin at Milwaukee in 1990 [Britannica Money](https://www.britannica.com/money/Satya-Nadella) and an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/satyanadella). His early ambition was a career in professional cricket, which he credits for teaching him about leadership [Stanford GSB](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/insights/microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-be-bold-be-right).

*   **Professional history & Financial connections**:
    Nadella is currently the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Microsoft [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella), a role he assumed in February 2014 [Microsoft Source](https://news.microsoft.com/source/exec/satya-nadella/). Before becoming CEO, he held various leadership positions within Microsoft, including his first executive role as Vice President of Microsoft Central by 1999 [Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-career-rise). Prior to joining Microsoft in 1992 [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella), he worked at Sun Microsystems [Britannica Money](https://www.britannica.com/money/Satya-Nadella).

    Financially, Nadella's total compensation was reported at $96.5 million in 2025 [mynorthwest.com](https://mynorthwest.com/money/microsoft-ceo-record-salary/4146257). As of March 1, 2024, he owned 801,331 shares of Microsoft Corp (MSFT), valued at approximately $344 million [LinkedIn Pulse](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/satya-nadella-stock-portfolio-areeba-rauf-0jwif). His personal investments include partial ownership in the Seattle Sounders FC, a Major League Soccer team [bbntimes.com](https://www.bbntimes.com/companies/meet-the-ceo-of-microsoft-satya-nadella-discover-his-net-worth-ethnicity-leadership-style-and-personal-life), and stakes in companies like Groww and Drata [Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/people/satya-nadella/__lQ_XlXSqrZ7K8eaou5pWUurCWbAbzuQBDoiKfThnma0).

*   **Behavioral patterns**:
    Nadella is recognized for his ethical leadership, which is credited with strengthening public trust in Microsoft [Reputation](https://blog.reputationx.com/investor-confidence-strategies). His stated mission, and that of Microsoft, is to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/satyanadella). Under his leadership, Microsoft has strategically shifted its focus towards cloud computing and artificial intelligence [Microsoft Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft), leading to significant stock growth [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2018/07/17/how-microsoft-has-evolved-under-satya-nadella.html). He has publicly testified in federal court, articulating Microsoft's position on competitive practices in the tech industry [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2023/10/03/microsoft-nadella-google-trial-search).
